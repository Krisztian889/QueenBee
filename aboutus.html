Queen Bee Cafe began with a simple mission â€“ to bring the best quality coffee and homemade honey possible to the locals and visitors. Organic products has always been at the heart of what we do. We focus on single-origin speciality-grade coffee, roasted by the talented folks at Queen Bee Cafe and carefully considered guest roasters. We offer two espressos and two filters every day, each different, each delicious.

We also offer "The beekeeping experience" that makes getting in a beesuit fun and gives a great insight into what goes on in the world of beekeeping. You will spend the day, working and learning one to one with the beekeeping team at Queen Bee Company.

Brunch is served in the morning and lunch in the afternoon. We source artisanal foods with local provenance wherever possible, and take as much care with our menu as we do with our coffee.

Many outside catering menu ideas are on the way for you business meetings or home parties and small events for when the covid19 measures come to an end.
