Our Honey Experience

Course Part 1

With a combination of hands-on experience, expert guidance and instruction this one-day course will give you the confidence and essentials skills to establish your own bee colony at home plus inspiration and ideas of how to use your honey. Your morning will be spent at Melrose Bee Farm, learning some essential theory before donning your protective suit and gaining some hands-on experience with our hives.

Course Part 2

The second part of the course will be to visit Melrose Bee Farm apiary to meet our bees and gain some hands-on practical experience with our colonies. Furthermore, you will visit a local apiary and have the chance to examine a beehive, with lessons on safety and beekeeping etiquette. Food is always the focus at Melrose, and a three-course Scottish lunch of local ingredients will be one of the highlights of your day.

Nature is everything

Queen Bee experiences are weather/season dependent. We stop running them at the begginging of November and recommence in Spring season of the following year.
