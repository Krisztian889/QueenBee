Beekeeping Courses & Experiences

The aim is to give you enough experience, theoretical knowledge and confidence to start your journey into beekeeping. You will spend the day, working and learning one to one with the beekeeping team at Queen Bee Co. in Melrose. As an extension to the beekeeping day course you can also get a starter package, which includes bees, hive, equipment and course. Everything you need to start beekeeping.
