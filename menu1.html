Our Unique Menu

Seasonality. This word means a lot to us. Our food and drinks offer is strongly influenced by the beautiful ingredients from Scotland’s larder, foraged daily and brought to us by our local suppliers. After spending months experimenting with unusual and interesting healthy flavour pairings we are proud to present the 2020 Winter Menu.

Brunch is served in the morning and lunch in the early afternoons. We source artisanal foods with local provenance wherever possible, and take as much care with our menu as we do with our coffee.

Many outside catering menu ideas are on the way for your business meetings and small events when the covid19 measures come to an end.

We truly hope you enjoy it!
